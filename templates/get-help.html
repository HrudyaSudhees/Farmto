Help Center & FAQ

Answers to your common questions.

How do I use the disease detector?

It's simple! On the "Get Started" page, either upload a photo or use your device's camera. Once the image appears, click the "Diagnose" button and wait a few moments for the AI to analyze the image and provide a diagnosis.

What kind of image gives the best results?

For the most accurate diagnosis, please use a photo taken in natural daylight. Ensure the leaf is in focus and fills a good portion of the frame. Avoid blurry images or photos with strong shadows.

What should I do after getting a diagnosis?

Our AI provides a likely diagnosis based on visual patterns. We recommend consulting with a local agricultural extension office for confirmation and to discuss the best treatment plan. You can also browse our shop for products that may help manage certain conditions.
