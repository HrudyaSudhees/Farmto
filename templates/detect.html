How to Get the Best Results

Follow these simple steps for the most accurate diagnosis.

Use Clear Lighting

Take photos in bright, natural daylight. Avoid harsh shadows that can hide details.

Avoid Blurry Images

Make sure the leaf is in focus and centered in the frame. Blurry images cannot be analyzed correctly.

Get the Right Distance

Hold your camera about 10-15 cm away from the leaf to capture enough detail without being too close.
